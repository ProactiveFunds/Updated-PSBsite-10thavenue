The standard section heading block: eyebrow, title and supporting line, left-aligned by default.

Use it to open any content section so headings stay on the system's type
scale and rhythm rather than being hand-rolled.

```jsx
<SectionHead eyebrow="Portfolio" title="Where the capital goes" sub="Every property we hold, mapped." />
<SectionHead title="How it works" align="center" />
```

`align="center"` also constrains the block to 46ch and centres it. For the
editorial serif treatment used on the home page, use `HomeSectionHead` with
`serif`.
